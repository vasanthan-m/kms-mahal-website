About Us

A Venue That Celebrates Every Occasion

KMS Mahal is an elegant and budget-friendly destination in the heart of Old Pallavaram, Chennai, crafted to host your most cherished social and auspicious events. With its spacious air-conditioned halls, tasteful décor, and top-notch amenities, every gathering here becomes a memory for a lifetime.

Conveniently located on the 200 Feet Radial Road opposite Narayana School, we offer two distinct venues — the grand KMS Mahal and the distinguished KMS Convention Hall — giving you the perfect space for any occasion, big or small.

KMS Mahal was founded in 2019 by K. Murugan, a man whose service-first mindset shaped everything we stand for. He carried the vision of this wedding hall for years before bringing it to life — driven by a belief that every business should serve the welfare of the people and help them step forward into the next chapter of their lives. That philosophy lives in every event we host.
